Research

Researching reliable AI-generated analytics under changing data.

Current direction: data lineage, provenance, selective recomputation, and validation of AI-generated analytical conclusions when datasets, schemas, or assumptions evolve.

Current research direction

Lineage-Aware Revalidation of AI-Generated Data Science Workflows Under Evolving Data. The goal is to track dependencies between data sources, transformations, SQL/Python workflows, models, visualizations, and textual claims so an AI analysis can be reused, partially recomputed, fully recomputed, or rejected safely after data changes.

Proposed benchmark: ChangeImpactBench for evaluating workflow validity after row, value, schema, label, and business-rule changes.
Core methods: dependency graphs, provenance tracking, selective recomputation, uncertainty-aware reuse/abstain decisions.
Target domains: finance, healthcare, e-commerce, logistics, and enterprise decision-making where stale analytics create risk.
